Udacians App – Help

 

Welcome to Udacians, the networking community by and for Udacity students. There’s a lot this community has to offer. Let’s quickly go through each of the main features to help you get started.

 

Editing your profile

 

Open the navigation drawer and select the “Edit Profile” button at the top of the screen. It’s recommended that you include a picture, title, and about section to help other Udacians find you. You can optionally include a link to your personal website, blog, LinkedIn, and Twitter accounts.

 

Connecting with Others

 

Like any other social network, you can share posts and follow other users. To write posts on your profile, simply tap the header at the top of the navigation drawer. To view another user’s profile, you can tap one of the blue icons on the map. Tap the orange button on the right of the screen to follow this user. Posts from users you follow appear on the “Feed” tab. You can view your list of followers and users following you on the “Connections” tab.

 

Posting Articles, Events, and Topics

 

Tap and hold one finger anywhere on the map and you can add articles, events, or topics. Articles open links to specific web pages. Events let you organize in-person or virtual events with other students. Topics let you ask questions and interact in a real-time chat.

 

Discussions

 

All topics you are participating in should appear on the “Discussions” tab. There are also group discussions for all students in each of your Nanodegree programs.

 

 Jobs

 

One of the biggest reasons to network is to help find a job. If you’re enrolled in a Nanodegree program, the “Jobs” tab shows job listings from Dice.com relevant to your skills. Don’t want to open the app to access job listings? Just add the Jobs widget to your home screen.
